Senior mechanical engineer with 20 years of experience developing complex electromechanical systems from concept to production release. Direct ownership of requirements definition, Verification & Validation strategy, design for manufacturability, multiphysics simulation, and PLM workflows on safety-critical hardware. Experienced in coordinating multidisciplinary teams (CFD, FEM, 1D, CAD, Testing & Validation, Sample Build) and managing supplier ecosystems across the full development cycle.


Currently Head of Advanced Engineering & Simulation at a fluid systems Tier-1, where I'm designing, building and testing for a high-pressure hydrogen systems programme. Earlier roles include Technical Lead on cross-country electric drive programmes at an automotive Tier-1 and structural/electromagnetic simulation, with initial aerospace experience on space opto-mechanical hardware.
